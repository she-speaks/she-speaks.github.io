Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to reside in London to join SheSpeaks?
Yes, our program is currently focused on London residents to ensure you can fully benefit from our:
  • Tutoring sessions
  • Local community events
  • London-specific resources and opportunities
However, if you're interested in online English learning but live outside London, please contact us! We're happy to recommend some great alternatives or discuss potential options.
Is the tutorial program completely free?

Yes, our tutoring program is entirely free for all learners. We believe that language learning should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

While we don't charge for our services, we're a non-profit organization that runs on grants and generous donations from our supporters. If you'd like to contribute to helping more women access English education, we welcome donations of any size.

Please contact us at shespeaks.contact@gmail.com to learn more about supporting our mission.
